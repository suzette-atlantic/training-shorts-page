The Science

The Science of Reinforcement

Why frequency beats duration

Science has a name for training going in one ear and out the other: the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve. Without reinforcement, up to 70% of new information vanishes within days. The antidote isn't longer training, it's frequent training.


Our micro-learning modules act as strategic speed bumps on the forgetting curve. By delivering bite-sized refreshers at regular intervals, we reset memory retention back to 100%, turning temporary compliance into long-term habit.
